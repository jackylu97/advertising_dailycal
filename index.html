The Daily Californian is an independent, student-run newspaper covering both the UC Berkeley campus and the city of Berkeley. Established in 1871, the Daily Cal is one of the oldest newspaper on the West Coast and one of the oldest college newspapers in the country. With a prominent and active student and community readership, the Daily Cal operates 24/7 news year round.
